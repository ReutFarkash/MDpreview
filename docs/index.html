Open any Markdown file
in Obsidian.

No vault required. Your theme, your fonts, your rendering — instantly.

$ open -a MDPreview README.md
Works on files outside any vault
Inherits your Obsidian theme and plugins
Right-click in Finder, double-click, or terminal

First run

  1. Drag MDPreview.app to Applications, then double-click to open.
    macOS will warn it was downloaded from the internet — click Open. Apple has scanned it.
  2. A dialog asks: "Would you like MDPreview to open .md files by default?"
    Click Set as Default. It may appear behind Obsidian's window — look behind the trust prompt if you don't see it.
  3. Obsidian opens and asks if you trust the vault — click Trust author and enable plugins.
